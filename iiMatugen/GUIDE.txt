## ii + Matugen Post Installation Guide

Your `ii` settings should be already configured.

To apply your color scheme and background, simply change your background or toggle light/dark mode within your `ii` Settings application.

### Applying SDDM Theme Settings:

If you only want to apply the SDDM theme settings you can run the provided script:

```bash
sudo ~/.config/hypr/custom/scripts/ii-sddm-theme/sddm-theme-apply.sh
```

### Additional ii-sddm-theme Configurations:

For further customization, check the `ii-sddm.conf` file located in this directory. Remember that after making changes to this file, you'll still need to adjust your background or light/dark mode in the `ii` settings app to ensure the new configurations
